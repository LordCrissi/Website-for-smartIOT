                    <td class="head"><span class="headlight_top">Welcome to SmartIoT 2026</span></td>
                </tr>
                <tr>
                    <td class="background-style"
                        style="PADDING-RIGHT: 10px; PADDING-LEFT: 10px; PADDING-BOTTOM: 10px; PADDING-TOP: 10px;">











                        <br>
                        <p align="justify" style="margin-top: -20px">Internet of Things (IoT) plays an important role in the current and future generation of information, network, and communication developing and applications. Smart IoT is an exciting emerging research field that has great potential to transform both our understanding of fundamental computer science principles and our standard of living. IoT is being employed in more and more areas making “Everything Smart”, such as smart home, smart city, intelligent transportation, environment monitoring, security systems, and advanced manufacturing. IEEE International Conference on Smart Internet of Things (IEEE SmartIoT) focuses on these challenges.</p>
                        <p align="justify">The 10th IEEE International Conference on Smart Internet of Things (IEEE SmartIoT 2026) will be held on 21-24, August 2026 at Shenyang, China. The primary goal of IEEE SmartIoT 2026 is to exchange, share and discuss the latest research, theories and applications for Smart Internet of Things and Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS). Prospective authors are invited to submit original research papers, which propose new methodologies, propose new research directions and discuss approaches or schemes for current existing issues. Topics of interests include, but are not limited to:</p>

                        <p align="justify">* Track 1. IoT Sensing, monitoring, networking and routing</p>
                        <p align="justify">* Track 2. Big data analysis and Cloud computing</p>
                        <p align="justify">* Track 3. Edge computing/Fog computing</p>
                        <p align="justify">* Track 4. Smart cities, intelligent transportation and internet of
                            vehicles</p>
                        <p align="justify">* Track 5. Artificial Intelligence, machine learning and evolutionary
                        Computing </p>
                        <p align="justify">* Track 6. Social networks, multimedia and mobile computing</p>
                        <p align="justify">* Track 7. Blockchain and emerging research or Technologies</p>
                        <p align="justify">* Track 8. Industrial 4.0 and Industrial IoT</p>
                        <p align="justify">* Track 9. Control and decision making for smart IoT or CPS</p>
                        <p align="justify">* Track 10. Security and privacy for smart IoT or CPS</p>
                        <br>
                       
                        <p>
                            Papers need to be prepared according to the IEEE format, and submitted in PDF format via via the<a
                            href="submission.jsp"> SmartIoT : 2026 submission site</a> (Submissions are now closed).</p>
                        <p>Accepted and presented papers will be included in the Conference Proceedings published
                            by CS Press (indexed by EI). At least one author of accepted papers MUST register and
                            present their work at the conference; otherwise, their papers will be removed from the
                            digital libraries of CS and EI after the conference. Authors of 50% papers will be
                            invited to submit their paper to a special issue in a SCI-indexed journal. Information about
                            these journal special issues are available on the website.

                        </p>
