ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

Where the physical and digital worlds converge.

Internet of Things plays an important role in current and future information, network and communication systems. Smart IoT is an exciting research field with the potential to transform both fundamental computer science and our standard of living.

IEEE SmartIoT 2026 brings researchers and practitioners together to exchange the latest research, theories and applications for Smart Internet of Things and Cyber-Physical Systems.
